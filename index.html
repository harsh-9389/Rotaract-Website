About The Club

Rotaract Club MNNIT (Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology) is a dynamic and compassionate committee of around 70 active volunteers dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of individuals and communities. Through various initiatives such as donations, orphanage home visits, blind school visits, and educational programs for young children, the Rotaract Club of MNNIT has been instrumental in fostering social change and creating a lasting impact.

The Rotaract Club of MNNIT proves that in today's world also humanity is alive and just needs a brushing up and the Club promises that it will serve the society like this forever.

Our Work

Blood Donation Camp

Blood Donation is the Principal activity of our Rotaract club. We have been pioneer in starting the idea of blood donation in Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad, Prayagraj.

We are also proud to share that our club has saved numerous lives inside and outside the institute through its blood donation drive. Whenever the fear of rising Dengue and Blood infections have caused terror among the crowd, it's the Rotaract Club of our institute which came forward to help the masses. 

Donation Drive

Remember that the happiest people are not those getting more, but those giving more. ―H. Jackson Brown Jr.

One of the key activities of the Rotaract Club MNNIT is organizing and facilitating donations to support underprivileged individuals and families.
Be it Cloth Donation, blanket Donation, bicycle donations or any other form of aid to the needy.We are proud to say that our Rotaract Family of MNNIT has distributed more than a thousand items till this very day for the benefits of our brothers and sisters in need. We are also glad that we have always been supported by the institute for this memorable cause. 

Disha Activity

DISHA ACTIVITY, an initiative by the volunteers of Rotaract Club MNNIT, is a remarkable endeavor aimed at providing education and recreational activities to underprivileged students of Bal Bharti School which is situated in the MNNIT Campus. This program goes beyond the conventional approach of teaching, incorporating interactive methods and engaging activities to enhance the overall learning experience. By focusing on both education and recreation, DISHA ACTIVITY creates a nurturing environment that empowers these students and opens up new horizons for their future. We are proud to say that DISHA ACTIVITY stands as a shining example of how education and recreation can transform the lives of underprivileged students.

Old Age Home, Blind School and Orphanage Visits

The Rotaract Club MNNIT believes in inclusive education and equal opportunities for all. To promote awareness and understanding of the challenges faced by visually impaired individuals, the club organizes visits to blind schools. Volunteers participate in interactive sessions, learn from the students, and engage in activities that enhance their understanding of the blind community. These visits help break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and foster empathy and inclusivity among club members
